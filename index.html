Voted "Best of Westchester" Six Times

Frankie & Fanucci’s in Mamaroneck, NY is a vibrant neighborhood pizzeria known for its award-winning, thin-crust pizzas baked in traditional wood-fired ovens. Our menu also features handmade pastas, fresh salads, and Italian favorites. With a full bar, handcrafted cocktails, and a family-friendly vibe, it’s the perfect spot for casual nights out or group gatherings.
