Our Story

A Vibrant
Neighborhood
Pizzeria

Frankie & Fanucci's in Mamaroneck, NY is more than a restaurant — it's the neighborhood's gathering place. Voted "Best of Westchester" six times, we've built our reputation on thin-crust perfection baked in traditional wood-fired ovens.

Our menu spans the full Italian table: handmade pastas, crispy antipasti, fresh salads, and entrées crafted with care. A full bar, handcrafted cocktails, and a family-friendly vibe make every visit memorable.
